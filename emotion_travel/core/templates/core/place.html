Kozhikode beach

Weather : 25 - 29°C

Timing : Nil

Entry fee : No Entry Fee

Overview

The beach is renowned for its tranquil charm, with breathtaking sunset views that paint the sky in vivid shades of orange and pink. Gentle waves and a refreshing sea breeze create a peaceful ambiance, making it a favorite spot for leisurely walks, picnics, and unwinding from the city's bustle.
